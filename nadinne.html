Who Are We?

I am Nadinne Chloe J. Montes, a grade 9 student from the Philippine Science High School Southern Mindanao Campus whose goal is to help alleviate the problems under-privileged families face. The Youth Poverty Advocacy Group (YPAG) is an association with a goal to spread awareness about the causes and effects of poverty as well as informing the public on the possible solutions that can be implemented. This group started in 2016 where numerous Philippine Science High School scholars saw the number of families who lived in poverty and decided to establish the YAPG with the core values of passion, empathy, and creating an inclusive community.
